AEP station naming convention

| Component | Station ID | Inlet ID | Outlet ID |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free Stream** | 0 | 0 | 0 |
| **Inlet (d)** | 1 – 2 | 1 | 2 |
| **Compressor (c)** | 2 – 3 | 2 | 3 |
| **Fan (f)** | 2 – 13 | 2 | 13 |
| **LP Compressor** | 2 – 2.5 | 2 | 2.5 |
| **HP Compressor** | 2.5 – 3 | 2.5 | 3 |
| **Combustor (b)** | 3 – 4 | 3 | 4 |
| **Turbine (t)** | 4 – 5 | 4 | 5 |
| **HP Turbine** | 4 – 4.5 | 4 | 4.5 |
| **LP Turbine** | 4.5 – 5 | 4.5 | 5 |
| **After burner (AB)** | 6 – 7 | 6 | 7 |
| **Duct Burner (DB)** | 16 – 17 | 16 | 17 |
| **Core Nozzle (n)** | 7 – 8 – 9 | 7 | 9 |
| **Fan Nozzle (fn)** | 17 – 18 – 19 | 17 | 19 |
| **Downstream Core** | 9 | 9 | 9 |
| **Downstream Fan** | 19 | 19 | 19 |
